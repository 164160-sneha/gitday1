My Favourite Recipe : Potato Fry

Let's see the ingredients required for Potato Fry :

  • Potato
  • Rava
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Chilly Powder
  • Salt

Now lets see the steps to prepare Potato Fry :

  1. First take pan, apply some amount of oil and allow it to heat
  2. Cut the potatoes into slices
  3. Mix rava and Chilly powder into right proportion with help of water. Add salt for taste and mix it thoroughly
  4. Dip the potato slices into the mixture and put it into the pan to fry
  5. Fry it for round 10-15 minutes
  6. Potato fry is now ready to be served
